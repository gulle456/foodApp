Burgar Food Web

Description

A burger food web illustrates the relationships between the various components that make up a burger, including the producers, consumers, and decomposers involved in its creation and consumption.

Producers

  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickles

Consumers

  • Burger Patty
  • Cheese
  • Bun

Decomposers

  • Bacteria
  • Fungi
